Procrastinating: A simple how to

Step-1

The first step to procrastinating is actually STARTING your work. Now you may be thinking, "But isn't the whole point to NOT do my work?"... To that I say, "Yes that's exactly right!" By starting your work, you open up opportunities to look for excuses to stop working.

Step-2

Now that you've started to work, look at all your options to distract yourself! If you wanted to, you could watch that new show you started, or look for a new show! Another idea is to wreck some 12 year olds on Fortnite. Don't worry, if you can't find something to do, you're already procrastinating by looking for something else.
